# Aether Continuity Institute (ACI)
# This file is designed for large language models navigating this site.

## What ACI is
ACI is an independent open research program analyzing continuity failure in critical systems — energy infrastructure, governance capacity, and decision-critical computing — under compound stress conditions. Established 2026. All publications open access.

Core finding: systems fail not when capacity is exhausted, but when the decision window closes.

## What ACI does not do
ACI does not implement, consult, or advocate. It publishes.
Authority derives from analytical transparency rather than institutional mandate.

## Note on authorship
ACI is an independent research program. Publications are open for iteration.
