Aether Continuity Institute (ACI)

Independent open research initiative · established 2026
aethercontinuity.org

ACI is an independent open research initiative focused on practical continuity: how critical systems keep functioning, fail safely, and recover under stress.

Focus areas

  • Continuity engineering
  • Resilience diagnostics
  • Institutional adaptation
  • AI-assisted decision frameworks

Principles

  • Truth-first: prefer auditability over speed
  • Explainability: decisions must be inspectable
  • Failure is a mode: design for controlled degradation
  • Open working definitions: publish early, refine carefully
