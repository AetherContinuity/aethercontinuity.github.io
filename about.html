Aether Continuity Institute (ACI)

Independent diagnostic research initiative · established 2026
aethercontinuity.org

ACI is an independent diagnostic research initiative studying how complex systems maintain operational continuity under compound stress — and the structural conditions under which decision capability fails despite nominal capacity availability.

Research Domains

  • D-1 Energy duration
  • D-2 Distributed continuity doctrines
  • D-3 Institutional decision capacity
  • D-4 Compound stress analysis
  • D-5 Continuity-oriented computing
  • D-6 Situational awareness persistence

Principles

  • Diagnostic clarity over solution advocacy
  • Analytical transparency as the basis of authority
  • Falsification conditions stated explicitly
  • Open working definitions: publish early, refine carefully
